The Maui Eldorado Set in the heart of Kaanapali .

Make your honeymoon or vacation in Hawaii complete in a luxurious and spacious resort condominium in beautiful MAUI. Set in the heart of Kaanapali, the Maui Eldorado has long been known as a premiere vacation destination. The only property to have its own private beach cabana, the Maui Eldorado stretches out along the Royal Kaanapali Golf Course at Black Rock, one of the finest snorkeling locations on Maui.
